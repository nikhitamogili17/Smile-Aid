Classification of Students Misconceptions in Individualized Learning Environment(C-SMILE). Here students are classified based on an assessment through which we can find out their learning analysis of the subject and give them correct motivation to improve,the assessment process is carried out by preparing two-level multiple-choice questions.Misconceptions are identified for each instructional objective to make a root question and linked questions.Depending on the answers provided by the student in first level, the linked questions are given to get more information regarding the misconception. Based on the first and second level answers of students, feedbacks are provided with the misconception identified.
